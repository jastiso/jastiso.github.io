Jennifer Stiso

Data scientist at Children's Hospital of Philadelphia. I've specialized in studying models of brain dynamics, and bias in academia annd medicine based on observed gender identity. Extensive experience with graph models, natural language processing, statistical models, and linear dynamical systems. Hopefully new specialties and expertise to come.
