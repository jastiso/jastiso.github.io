Jennifer Stiso

Data Science Manager at SmarterDx. I've spent the last few years building machine learning solutions for hospital revenue cycle management. The revenue cycle is essentially a complex interlinked system of different teams that make sure hospitals are paid by insurance companies for the care they provide. Previously, I've specialized in studying brain dynamics, the healthcare revenue cycle, gender bias in academia and medicine, and cardiac ICU intervention prediction. Extensive experience with deep learning, graph models, machine learning, statistical models, and linear dynamical systems. Hopefully new specialties and expertise to come.
